# Lottery and Prize Claim Scams

## Overview
Lottery scams notify users that they have won a lucky draw, cash prize (e.g., KBC Lottery), or high-value gift (e.g., luxury car). To dispatch the reward, the scammer requests advance payments for customs clearances, bank processing fees, or registration taxes via UPI.

## Warning Signs
*   **Unsolicited Win Alerts**: Notifications of winning lotteries or sweepstakes that you never entered.
*   **Tax/Processing Fee Demands**: Requirements to pay upfront fees to release the prize money.
*   **Urgent claims**: Demanding payment immediately before the prize "expires".

## Safe Practices
*   **No Free Wins**: You cannot win a lottery you did not buy a ticket for.
*   **Tax directly withheld**: Real prizes have taxes deducted at the source (TDS); they are never paid via individual UPI addresses.
*   **National Guidelines**: Consult NPCI security directives regarding lottery UPI payments.
